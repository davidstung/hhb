FAQ
What is your Privacy Policy?
When you decide to use Happy Healthy Better you trust us with your information. This privacy policy is meant to help you understand what data we collect and what we do with it. This is important, we hope you read it through. Let's start with what we will never do. - We will never contact you with unsolicited information that does not align with helping you make sense of the healthcare system. - We will never sell data tied to your identity. Happy Healthy Better helps un-complicate the healthcare system (doctors and hospitals and your insurance) when you need to use it. In order to do this we initially ask for the following information: First and Last Name - We ask for your first and last name so that we are able to engage in a conversation with you about our Private Beta. We feel that it is important to treat each user as a person instead of a number. When you are invited to participate in our Private Beta, your First and Last Name will also be used to provide you with your benefit information. Email- We ask for your email as this will be how we invite you into our Private Beta. We will never contact you with information that is not aligned with our mission to un-complicate healthcare. Email will be the primary form of communication and how you will log in to our Private Beta. Phone- We ask for a phone number because most of the times it is easier to speak to an actual person. If you need help with healthcare you can call us. This is also optional. By submitting your information you are indicating that you are interested in the services we provide (clear and simple information about your healthcare benefits, healthcare cost tracking and comparison and yearly healthcare rewards program). In order for Happy Healthy Better to provide you with accurate and tailored information about your healthcare we will be be working with your insurance company to collect information on your plan information and service cost information. In order to do this we will need your log in information for your insurance provider. Our system anonymizes your healthcare information so that it is never tied to your identity except when the information is communicated to you. Please contact us below with any questions or concerns.
How can you help me save money?
Many healthcare plans in the United States have a deductible or an amount of money that you need to pay before your benefits start to kick in. For many, this amount is often in the thousands of dollars. The power as a consumer is in your hands, but unfortunately the information is not. Happy Healthy Better researches the providers in your area (doctors and hospitals) and presents clear and understandable information to make the best decision possible. This information could mean the difference between a $25 charge and a $2000 charge.
Why do you need my insurance information?
We believe accuracy is important. We don't want to recommend a doctor that is out of your network or have your doctor send you an unexpected bill. In order to do this, we use your insurance information to gather accurate details about the current status of your health plan.
Do you save my information?
We only securely save your information if you create an account to book an appointment.
